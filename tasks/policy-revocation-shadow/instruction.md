The JSON has a top-level cases array matching the supplied traces. Each case reports name, ok, decisions, and stale. Each decision reports label, principal, resource, action, verdict, epoch, reused, batch_id, and delegated_from. The batch_id field carries the active group name from grouped checks and is empty otherwise. The delegated_from field names the grantor for inherited rows and is empty for direct rows. Inherited access walks the delegation chain from the requesting subject toward grantors; if any subject along that walk is blocked or revoked, the inherited row denies even when the requesting subject itself is still active. A blocked or revoked subject never receives allow on delegated paths. Each decision epoch is the subject epoch at evaluation time; epochs are nondecreasing inside each case. Repeated equivalent checks mark reused for stable subjects. A clean audit keeps every case clean, stale empty, and matrix reruns aligned.
